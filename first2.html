First human spaceflights

Russian cosmonaut (Yuri Gagarin) was the first man in space and he orbited Earth on 12th April, 1961. The first woman in space was Russian cosmonaut (Valentina Tereshkova) on 16th June, 1963. The first man on moon was USA astronaut (Neil Armstrong), in 1969.
