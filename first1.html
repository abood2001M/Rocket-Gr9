First creatures in space

The first living creature in space were flies. they were sent by the USA to the edge of space in a rocket in 1947 and were recovered alive. A succession of test flights involving animals came after, including dogs, cats, monkeys, chimpanzees, guinea pigs, frogs, tortoises, rats and various fish. Unfortunately, not all came back alive.
